ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Interested students who have completed or appeared their higher secondary final exams can directly apply to the college with their academic certificates, citizenship certificate or passport or birth certificate or citizenship of either of the parents. The students awaiting results can get provisional admissions. Once at the college, the admission team will assess the entry requirement mentioned below and assist in admission process.
Applicants should be minimum 18 years of age and should possess the minimum qualifications mentioned here under.

For BSc (Computer Science)

  • Academic Level
    • Completion of NEB +2 Qualification with an aggregate ≥ 55% or 2.2 CGPA*.
    • For A-Levels, a minimum of 3.5 credits and atleast a grade of D and above.
  • English Proficiency
    • English NEB XII Marks ≥ 60% or 2.4 GPA.
    • Pass in General Paper or English Language or IELTS 5.5 or PTE 47.

Applicants not meeting the aforementioned criteria for English can demonstrate
their English proficiency with the following internationally recognised English
Tests:

    • English NEB XII Marks ≥ 60% or 2.4 GPA.
    • Pass in General Paper or English Language or IELTS 5.5 or PTE 47.

Students not meeting the aforementioned criteria will have to pursue Year 0
(Level 3), an additional one year academic program from The University of
Wolverhampton, UK.

For BSc (International Business Mangement)

  • Academic Level
    • Completion of NEB +2 Qualification with an aggregate ≥ 45% or 1.8 CGPA.
    • For A-Levels, a minimum of 3.5 credits and atleast a grade of D and above.
  • English Proficiency
    • English NEB XII Marks ≥ 45% or 1.8 GPA.
    • Pass in General Paper or English Language or IELTS 5.5 or PTE 47.

Applicants who do not meet the aforementioned English requirements can
substitute with the following standardised English Tests:

    • English NEB XII Marks ≥ 60% or 2.4 GPA.
    • Pass in General Paper or English Language or IELTS 5.5 or PTE 47.

All other qualifications (CBSE, IB, AP, etc) are evaluated on a case-by-case basis by The University of Wolverhampton.

* the above entry requirements in GPA & CGPA are only used as a reference and are subject to change pertaining to the official information from NEB and or the Ministry of Education, Nepal.
